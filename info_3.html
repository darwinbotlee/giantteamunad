Latest News

The 2016 Enduro World Series kicked off over the weekend in South America, and the Giant Factory Off-Road Team put in a strong showing over two days of intense competition on the Patagonian coastline of Chile.

Frenchman Yoann Barelli led the way with a sixth-place finish, and Aussie Josh Carlson racked up some solid points finishing 11th overall.

The Monteanbaik Enduro World Series in the village of Corral tested riders with a combination of ancient paths and freshly cut singletrack trails with views of the Pacific Ocean. The event was threatened when a forest fire broke out in the hills above Corral the day before the race, but in the end it went off as scheduled with three stages held Saturday and three more on Sunday.
