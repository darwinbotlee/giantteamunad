Latest News

The Giant Factory Off-Road Team stormed the enduro event at the Sea Otter Classic Thursday in Monterey, California, taking two podium spots and putting five riders in the top-10 of the elite men’s race.

Australian Josh Carlson led the way, racing his Anthem Advanced bike to a second-place finish behind fellow Aussie Jared Graves.

“The Sea Otter enduro is always a super tough race,” Carlson said. “The stages are quite simple but it’s hard to piece together four perfect stages. I was stoked with how I performed today and really happy to come away with second place.”
